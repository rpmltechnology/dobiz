ESIC Register

The Employees' State Insurance Corporation, an insurance based on need scheme that was incorporated into the Employees" State Insurance Act to provide better medical care for employees, workers as well as their immediate family members.

The scheme safeguards the interests of employees in the event of emergencies, including the occurrence of sickness, maternity, death from injury or injury permanent or temporary physical disability and anything that impacts the capacity to earn.

The act also provides a broader scope of employers an exemption from their obligations or responsibilities as a result of the Workmen Compensation Act 1923 and Maternity Benefit Act, 1961.

Checklist to LLP Registration

STEP 1- ESIC COVERAGE

Any establishment that employs more than 10 employees or in the previous years hired more than 10 people; then the establishment is legally required to register and offer the benefits of ESIC for its employees.

STEP 2 - WAGE LIMIT

Employees who earn a salary of more than Rs. 21,000/- are qualified for ESIC coverage. But, to determine the employees employed by the establishment, the entire workforce is covered.

STEP 3 - CONTRIBUTION RATE

Employers are required to contribute 3.25 percent of the total wages paid to employees, while the contribution of employees is 0.75 percent of their total wage. The obligation to deduct the contribution of the employee lies with the employer.
