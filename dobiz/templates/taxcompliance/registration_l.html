Registration licenses

Shops & Establishment

The store and status quo act governs work hours, safety, and conditions for personal zone workers. In India, the registration process is compulsory and must be completed by the local labor officer. Non Compliance is a Crime.

Trade License

The municipality regulates trading activity to safeguard the neighborhood and prevent nuisances to the people living around the trading concern. It is compulsory, and a different fee is collected depending on the activity.

Register for GST

Supply of Goods and Supply, which are taxable events under GST, require that all persons involved in the supply or collection of GST be liable to the GST. To follow the above GST Registration, the taxpayer must file a periodical GST Return.

Export and Import Code

Import and export codes are unique identifications of individuals registered with the. DGFT. These are prerequisites for conducting an Indian Import/Export trade. IEC may be acquired in opposition to a single pan. Only one IEC is possible against one PAN.

License to Use Drugs

You will need a wholesale or retail license to trade in pharmaceutical goods. Many pharmacy companies market and sell their products after receiving them from a third-party manufacturer.

PSARA

Private security guards are in high demand. Before the agency can begin a business, it must first get a License. The license may be issued for one district or several districts in the state.
