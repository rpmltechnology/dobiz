FSSAI renewal

Because they deal directly with essential elements of human life, such as food, the business of food business operators (FBOs) is regulated. Food. This regulation is the Food Safety and Standards Regulations (Licensing and Registering of Food Businesses), Regulations 2011. It states that FBOs must seek registration or License from FSSAI before they can carry out their food business activities. FSSAI allows enrollment in three categories. These are

FSSAI Registry: This registration is for small businesses like Dabbawalas. Or petty PFO with less than 12 Lakhs in turnover

FSSAI License for Medium-Sized Businesses with more than 12 lakhs in annual turnover.

Central FSSAI Certificate: Food Central License is required by FBOs operating in more states or with an annual turnover greater than 20 crores.

Validity and Registration of License

Have you checked the FSSAI food license or registration certificates? Check your food registration certificate or food license issued by FSSAI. The food license or registration is not permanent. A food license or registration is valid for one to five years, depending on the choice made by the Food Business Operator when applying. The validity period started when the License or registration certificate was issued. FBOs can apply to renew their Food License before expiry if they wish to continue their Food Business. You can renew for up to five more years by filing a renewal request. But you must pay the fee applicable to that period.

When to Apply for Renewal FSSAI Registration/License

FBO should apply to renew a Fssai Registration or License granted under these Regulations at least 30 days before the License expires. An applicant may file a renewal request 120 days in advance.

Each day of delay in filing a renewal request must be paid Rs 100.

Repercussions of FSSAI Registration/License Non-Renewal

The license expiry must be notified if the renewal request is not received within the prescribed time. In such a circumstance, the Food Business Operator may stop all business activity at the Food Unit. The Food Business Operator must apply for a new registration or food license if it wants to restart.

Designated officers can request an inspection before renewal if they feel it is necessary. The Food Business Operator does not need to be inspected if they have a valid certificate from an accredited agency by the Food Authority or a food safety auditor accredited by FSSAI.
