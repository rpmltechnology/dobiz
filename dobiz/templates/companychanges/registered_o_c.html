Procedure for the Change in Registered Office Address

New Address to be Identified

In compliance with Local Land Rules, the process involves the identification of a new registered address for the company.

The company should only occupy new premises after executing the appropriate document, such as a sale deed or rent agreement.

Ask for a Notification of Compliance from the Owner

A NOC must be obtained from the property owner if the new address isn't yet owned. Board Meeting to Pass Resolutions

At a properly convened meeting of directors, adopt a Board resolution for change of registered address with a simple majority.

Filing INC-22 to Get Intimate ROC

Notify the registrar of any change within 30 days of effective change. You can do this by filing Form INC-22.

Change All Stationary & Sign Board

After changing the registered address, the company will need to make the necessary changes to all signs and marketing materials.
