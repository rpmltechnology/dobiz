Designs Registration Online in India

Registration and protection for industrial design in India are governed through the Designs Act, 2000 and the Designs Rules, 2001, which was in force on 11 May 2001, repealing the Act of 1911. Design Rules, 2001 was further amended by the Design Rules, 2001 was later amended with the help of Designs (Amendment) Rules 2008 and Designs (Amendment) Rules 2014. The most recent design rules amendment was enacted on 30 December 2014. It included the category of applicants as small entities in addition to natural persons and any other entity that is not a small one.

Procedure for Design Registration Documentation and Preparation

Regarding the Design registration, the initial step is to create the necessary documents to make it available to the department. The documents include the Design questionnaire, description of the class and the nature of the Design, colour images of the Design, and Power of Attorney in Form-21.

Design Specification Drafting Design Specification

A Design can be described as a 3-dimensional design of objects, products such as equipment, automobile parts, for example. Based on the appearance. It must be written down in a specification, which requires knowledge and knowledge. After drawing the design specification, the specification should be filed.

The Filing of Design Applications

The Design application must be made online and signed by the Attorney on every page of the design specification. After the submission is successful, the payment of Rs. 1000/- for filing fees must be made, resulting in the issuance of an acknowledgment.

Reply to the report of examination and any objections.

The last stage is to respond to and rectify any objections made by the department's Design department. If the department is satisfied, it will issue the certificate of Design registration.

Departmental Objection Handling

The last stage is to respond to and rectify any objections made from departmental objections raised by the Design department. If the department is satisfied, they will issue an official certificate of Design registration valid for 10 years. This can be extended to five years.

Design Registration

The Design Registration Certificate is granted from the Design Office after objections, if any were overcome, at the pleasure of the Design Office.
