Filing Director KYC 2022 (DIN-3 KYC), with ROC

Director's KYC Filing, an annual task, applies to any person who was allocated a DIN (Director Identity Number) before 31 March 2022. DIR-3 KYC forms must be filed to the ROC to maintain the records of the ROC with the correct Address, phone number and email address of directors/designated associates. It is mandatory to file the form within the deadline of October 2022. If filed before that date, there is no charge from the government. Failure to file KYC within the due date will result in the deactivation of DIN numbers. This can be done by filing DIR-3 KYC and late filing fees at Rs. 5000 for each defaulting Director or designated partner.

Filing the DIR-3 KYC form to ROC

The Director must sign the form electronically, and the certificate must be certified by a practicing CS, CS, or CMA. A NIL fee is required if the DIR-3 form (KYC) is not filed by the deadline. An extra fee of Rs. is required if the due dates have passed. For each person who has a DIN, an extra Rs. 5000 will be charged.

Web-based Online DIN KYC

If the DIN KYC has been filed in the past year by a person, and his details (such as a mobile number, residential address and email) have not changed in any way, the Director KYC can be filed in a web-based portal with OTP-based authentication. The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has made it possible to file the Director KYC using a web-based service by inserting a clause in Rule 12A of 2018, Companies (Appointment of Qualification of Directors) Regulations, 2018. The web-based filing doesn't require you to upload or submit any document. The Director must ensure that the Director's information is accurate and not changed.

Note: Let's say there was a change to the Director's home address. This is when the Director should file a DIR-6 with all relevant documents. The ROC will update the records for the DIN Holder.

The director KYC's web-based facility is unavailable for the first-time filer. After certification by a qualified professional, these individuals must file the DIN KYC and upload the DIR-3 KYC form.
