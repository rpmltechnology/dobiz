IPR Registration

Trademark registration

A trademark can distinguish one company from the other. Any sign, word or mark identifying the origin of goods, services, or products is a trademark. Every business has several trademarks. We will help you to register and enforce them.

International Trademark Filing

One country's laws only apply to its territory. International applicability is not possible. Under the Madrid Convention, an international trademark application can also be filed from India to protect your trademark in other countries.

Copyright Register

The right to any music, literature, cinematography or code software work rests with the creator. As conclusive evidence of the copyright owner, the registrar keeps a copyright register and issues a certificate.

Patent Registration

Patents may be issued in the name and designation of the inventor, assigned applicant, for inventions of goods or Industrial Processes that are unique or have not been used before. The patent gives you the exclusive right to use it or authorizes others to do so for twenty years. We file patents in hours upon receipt of all documentation.

International Patent File

International protection of your invention can be secured by filing an International Patent Application within 12 months of filing it in India. The Indian Patent Office must be used to file the PCT Application. It is a Nodal Agency under the PCT.

Design Registration

India considers three-dimensional designs of goods to be intellectual property. This right can be protected through a design registration application. There is only one condition: it must not be novel but also have a distinctive element and innovation.
